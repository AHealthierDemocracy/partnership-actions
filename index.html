About Vot-ER

Vot-ER is a nonpartisan, nonprofit integrating civic engagement into healthcare settings through its tools, programs, and coaching. By focusing on voting as a social determinant of health, Vot-ER helps patients and healthcare professionals use their voices at the ballot box to address the underlying factors driving health inequities.
