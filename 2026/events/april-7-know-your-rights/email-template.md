Your members, chapter leaders, mailing list, or any healthcare workers in your network who are navigating ICE activity in their settings.

## What to Customize

- `[ASSOCIATION NAME]` — your organization's name
- `[FIRST NAME]` — or replace with a group salutation (e.g., "Dear [ORG] community")
