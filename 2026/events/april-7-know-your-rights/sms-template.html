SMS segments cap at 160 characters each. Messages over 160 send as multi-part and may incur additional carrier costs. This template stays within 2 segments. Always verify your final character count in your SMS platform before sending.

What to customize

  • [RSVP LINK] — use a shortened URL (e.g., via bit.ly) to save characters
  • Opening (optional) — prefix with your org name for recognizability if recipients may not have your number saved (e.g., "From [ORG]: Healthcare workers…")
  • Opt-out language — add "Reply STOP to unsubscribe" as a follow-up message or appended to the send (required by TCPA)
