# Legal & Compliance Notes

> These flags were identified in a review of the partner templates. Consult your organization's legal counsel before sending, particularly regarding CAN-SPAM and TCPA compliance.

## Email (CAN-SPAM)

- **Physical mailing address required:** Add your organization's address to the email footer before sending.
- **Unsubscribe mechanism:** Confirm your bulk email platform adds an unsubscribe link automatically.
- **Sender identification:** The "From" name must accurately identify your organization. Do not send as Vot-ER unless explicitly authorized.
- **Subject line:** Accurate and non-deceptive. No changes needed.
- **Content:** Nonpartisan, no fundraising, no legal promises. No content-level concerns.

## SMS / Text (TCPA)

- **Prior express written consent required:** Confirm your list was collected with appropriate opt-in language.
- **Opt-out language missing:** Add opt-out instructions (e.g., "Reply STOP to unsubscribe") — as a follow-up message or appended to the send.
- **Sender identification missing:** Consider prefixing with `[ORG NAME]:`.
- **Platform compliance:** If using a peer-to-peer texting platform (Hustle, ThruText, etc.), confirm their TCPA compliance policies apply to your send.
- **Content:** Factual, nonpartisan, no false claims. No content-level concerns.

## General

- **Nonpartisan framing:** Both templates are nonpartisan. Appropriate for 501(c)(3) organizations.
- **Event accuracy:** Confirm date, time, and RSVP link are correct before sending.
- **Hosting attribution:** Do not modify the hosting attribution line without confirmation from Vot-ER.
