## Description

ICE enforcement in healthcare settings is driving patients away from care. This training gives healthcare workers the legal grounding and practical tools to protect patient trust and access — right now.

## Learning Objectives

- Rights and responsibilities for healthcare workers during ICE encounters
- Workplace preparation and response strategies for ICE activity
- Tools to share with patients so they feel safer accessing care

## Audience

Healthcare professionals, students, and support staff navigating immigration enforcement in medical facilities.
