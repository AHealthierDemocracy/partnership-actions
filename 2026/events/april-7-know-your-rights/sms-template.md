## Character Note

Approximately 215 characters without the URL. A standard shortened URL (~22–25 characters) brings the total to ~240 — within a 2-segment send. Always verify with your SMS platform before sending.

SMS messages are capped at 160 characters per segment. Messages over 160 characters send as multi-part (MMS) and may incur additional carrier costs.
